OUR MISSION

We remain focussed in our mission of community building by establishing institutes of learning (schools, colleges, residential hotels) that focus on imparting holistic education We believe Taleem and Tarbiyah are the cornerstones that help produce resilient, grounded, balanced and accomplished children embedded with strong ethical and moral values. We believe that community building work is not complete until poor and vulnerable are honoured and given equal opportunities to excel.


COMMUNITY

We work hard in engaging community by grass root contact and by monthly community events for families across multiple cities in England, Scotland and Wales.
We also conduct weekly sports activities for children. We aim to build families that are not only empowered with holistic education, wisdom and best character but also raise awareness about taking responsibility of building community by active participation. Creating awareness within the families about their responsibilities is the key for a sustained growth and building next generation of leaders to carry the work forward
