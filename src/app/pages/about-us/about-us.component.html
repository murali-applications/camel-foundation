About Us

Camel Foundation

Camel Foundation is a UK based international NGO which provides support to developmental work across the world predominantly in India. We believe in establishing Táaleem (education) and Tarbiyah (nurturing) as a cornerstone to achieve our objective. Camel Foundation strives towards honouring the vulnerable and poor section of the society by helping them to unleash their potential and empower them with a holistic approach towards education – a key tool to transform their lives. Camel Foundation with its team of volunteers are guided by the principles of Islam which stands for universal peace, equality and honouring the weak and vulnerable sections of our community. We care to tell you that we do not encourage any particular school of thought in Islam, and any political activities are strictly unfavored. We are steadfast, honest and unwavering in our objective of transforming our community

Creating awareness:


Awareness raising is an important tool to generate support , participation and sustenance of work , along with building a healthy society around ourselves

CFT doesn’t believe that donating for community building is enough. This is why CFT volunteers regularly engage individuals, families and their children and the wider populace in community through outreach work. Our focus is on encouraging people to be conscious of the responsibilities as a believer and engage in developing self , their family and the community . Encourage individuals and families to become aware of the needs of wider community. To respect and honour the poor and vulnerable section of the community by giving them equal opportunities to reach their full potential.


Community building work is a team work. It can’t be achieved by few people.

Working with partners:

CFT doesn’t believe in telling tall-tales or make big claims. We know our limits, but we also know that united, there’s nothing we cannot change. CFT looks forward to engage and partner with other NGOs with a similar vision. Currently we partner with Camel foundation – India and Camel foundation – USA.
